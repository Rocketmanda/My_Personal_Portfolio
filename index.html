About Me

I'm a passionate Developer who's part of the incredible She Codes Plus Brisbane Program 2023 (May - October 2023). Armed with the languages I learned during the course (HTML, CSS, Python, React JS, and Django), I'm on a mission to create awesome digital experiences.

It's not all about tech and travel. I find solace in reading good books or getting my culinary-creative on in the kitchen.
